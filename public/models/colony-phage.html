Colony growth

One phage is seeded onto a surface cell when the colony first reaches N.
Founder divides at exactly this time; later divisions ~ Gamma(mean, form factor 10). Infected cells stop dividing.
Thermal noise in the damped Langevin motion (0 = quiet, the default).
Mobility on e−ax−e−ax/2 (a = ln2, rest length 2, cutoff 2.5).

Phage kinetics

2D diffusion of a bound (not-yet-injected) phage on the cell surface. Lower it to watch the surface walk clearly.
Surface-bound phage commits the cell to infection at this rate.
Eclipse + maturation ~ Gamma(mean, form factor 10), then lysis.
Phages released into the lysed cell's volume, then it is removed.

View
