Survival fraction: of genera alive at time t, the fraction that exist just after the event. Conditional survival: of those that reach the event, the fraction that survive it. MSD: mean-square model–data gap of the conditional curve, averaged back to where <5 genera survive to the event, over 4 noise runs. Source: J. J. Sepkoski Jr. genus compendium (ISC 2000): “No recent” = extinct genera only; “With recent” also includes genera still living today (LAD = 0).
